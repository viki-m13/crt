Exact Execution Instructions
STEP 1 — DETERMINE REGIME (daily at close)
At 4:00 PM ET, check TWO conditions:
A. Is SPY above its 50-day simple moving average (SMA50)?
B. Are 55%+ of sector ETFs above their own SMA50? (count how many of the 9 sectors are above their own 50-day SMA)
BULL regime (both A and B true for 3+ consecutive days):
Allocate: 40% SPY + 60% in momentum-weighted sector portfolio (see Step 2).
BEAR/MIXED regime (either A or B false):
Allocate: 100% defensive sectors (XLU, XLP, XLV), weighted by inverse volatility.
Always invested — never cash. Regime requires 3 consecutive days to confirm a switch (hysteresis).
STEP 2 — SECTOR ALLOCATION (monthly rebalance)
Bull regime: momentum-weighted sectors
On the 1st trading day of each month (or on regime change to BULL):
1. Compute composite momentum score for each sector: 25% × 21d z-score + 50% × 63d z-score + 25% × 126d z-score
2. Keep only sectors with positive composite score
3. Weight by inverse volatility (risk-parity) among the positive sectors
4. These sectors get the 60% allocation; SPY gets 40%
Bear/Mixed regime: defensive allocation
Hold XLU, XLP, XLV weighted by inverse 21-day volatility (lower vol gets more weight).
This naturally tilts toward the least-volatile defensive sector.
STEP 3 — VOL TARGETING
Scale total position to target 10% annualized portfolio volatility.
Scale factor = 10% ÷ (trailing 42-day realized vol). Max scale: 2.5×.
In calm markets: full or slightly levered. In volatile markets: reduced position.
