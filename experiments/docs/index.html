STEP 1 — CHECK ASSET CLASS TRENDS (monthly)
Check 3 asset classes. Each is "trending" if above its 50-day SMA:
1. SPY (equities) above SMA50?
2. TLT (long-term bonds) above SMA50?
3. GLD (gold) above SMA50?
1+ trending: Equal-weight allocation across trending classes. Equity portion: top 20 quality-momentum stocks.
0 trending: Defensive fallback: 50% IEF + 50% GLD.
STEP 2 — STOCK SELECTION (equity portion)
When equities trending: pick top 20 stocks
Filter: above 200-day SMA AND positive 252d return (quality gate).
Score: risk-adjusted momentum = (40% × 63d return + 60% × 126d return) ÷ 63d volatility.
Hold top 20 scored stocks, equally weighted within the equity allocation.
Turnover reduction
Existing stocks that remain in top 40 are kept. Only replace stocks that fall out of the top 40.
STEP 3 — VOL TARGETING & REBALANCING
Scale total position to target 10% annualized portfolio volatility.
Scale = 10% ÷ trailing 42-day realized vol. Max: 3×.
Rebalance every ~21 trading days (monthly).
Costs: 5 bps slippage + 5 bps transaction per trade
