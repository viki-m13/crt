Execution Instructions
STEP 1 — SIGNAL DETECTION (daily)
Each day at close, the system scans ~100 stocks for MTMDI spikes (momentum dispersion across 6 timeframes).
MTMDI z-score > 1.5 + direction positive: Short-term momentum recovering while long-term is depressed — resolution imminent.
No signal: Stay flat. Only trade when dispersion is unusually high (>93rd percentile).
STEP 2 — CONFIRMATION (CACS + MPR)
Require at least one confirming factor beyond MTMDI:
• CACS (Cross-Asset Cascade Score): stock lagging its market-expected move by >2%
• MPR (Momentum Persistence Ratio): momentum accelerating (z-score > 0.5)
• Signal strength = 50% MTMDI + 30% CACS + 20% MPR
STEP 3 — SIZING + EXITS
Position size: vol-targeted (15% target / stock vol) × strength × regime factor.
• Max 5% per stock, 80% total exposure
• High-vol regime: reduce size by 50%
• Exit: MTMDI collapses (z < 0.5), stop loss -8%, take profit +20%, or 63-day max hold
Execute at next-day open with 10bps slippage.
