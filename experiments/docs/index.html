How to Execute
Exact Execution Instructions
STEP 1 — CHECK EVERY DAY at market close
At 4:00 PM ET, look up SPY's closing price and its 30-day simple moving average (SMA30).
If SPY CLOSED ABOVE SMA30:
You should be invested. If you are already invested, do nothing. If you are in cash (from a previous exit), proceed to Step 2 to re-enter.
If SPY CLOSED BELOW SMA30:
Sell everything at the next day's open. Sell both your SPY position AND your sector ETF position. Go to 100% cash.
STEP 2 — PICK THE SECTOR (two scenarios)
Scenario A: 1st trading day of a new month
If SPY is above SMA30, look up the 3-month (63 trading day) total return for each of these 9 sector ETFs: XLK, XLF, XLE, XLV, XLI, XLY, XLP, XLU, XLB. Highest return = your sector pick for the month.
Allocate: 60% SPY + 40% winning sector ETF.
If you held a different sector, swap it. Keep SPY.
Scenario B: SPY just crossed BACK above SMA30
Can happen any day. At next day's open, buy:
• 60% SPY
• 40% whichever sector ETF has the highest 3-month return
Same 60/40 split. Full re-entry with best sector.
What does NOT trigger a sector change
Mid-month sector leadership changes — you do nothing. Sector only changes on the 1st of month or at re-entry after cash.
ALLOCATION & COSTS
When invested: 60% SPY + 40% top sector ETF
