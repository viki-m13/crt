How to Execute
Exact Execution Instructions
STEP 1 — ON THE 1ST TRADING DAY OF EACH MONTH
At market close, compute the momentum acceleration for each sector ETF:
accel = (21-day return today) − (21-day return 10 days ago)
Rank all sectors by acceleration. Pick the top 3 with positive acceleration.
Allocate equally: 33.3% in each of the top 3 sector ETFs.
Place Market-On-Close (MOC) orders to swap any changed sectors.
STEP 2 — DAILY EXPOSURE ADJUSTMENT (optional)
Compute trailing 21-day portfolio volatility (annualized). Scale total exposure:
exposure = min(100%, 15% / realized_vol)
High vol → reduce positions. Low vol → full positions. This is automatic crash protection.
ALLOCATION & COSTS
Always invested (no binary timing gate). Exposure scales with vol target.
Sector ETFs: XLK, XLF, XLE, XLV, XLI, XLY, XLP, XLU, XLB, XLRE, XLC
