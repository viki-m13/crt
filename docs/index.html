The Core Question

"I have money to invest today. What stocks should I buy that will most likely be profitable in 1, 3, and 5 years?"

This tool provides evidence-based answers using historical data and validated backtesting. For each stock, we show the probability of positive returns and probability of beating SPY based on similar past situations.

What Makes a Stock Score High?

Our composite score weights factors by their proven ability to predict alpha:

  • Momentum (40%) — Stocks going up tend to keep going up. This is the strongest predictor.
  • Trend (25%) — Price above key moving averages (SMA50, SMA200).
  • Quality (20%) — Consistent monthly gains, lower volatility.
  • Historical (15%) — How similar past setups performed vs SPY.

How to Read the Numbers
  • Score (0-100) — Composite ranking. Higher = better outlook.
  • P(+1Y), P(+3Y), P(+5Y) — Probability of positive return at each horizon.
  • Beat SPY — Probability of outperforming SPY index.
  • Median Return — Typical return from similar historical situations.
  • Downside / Upside — 10th and 90th percentile outcomes.
  • Confidence — Based on sample size of historical matches.
Signals
  • STRONG_BUY — Score ≥70, P(+3Y) ≥75%, Beat SPY ≥55%
  • BUY — Score ≥60, P(+3Y) ≥70%
  • HOLD — Score ≥45, P(+1Y) ≥60%
  • AVOID — Does not meet above criteria
