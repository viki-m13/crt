What this does

  • Measures washout (0–100) for each stock: how “sold‑off / stressed” it looks relative to itself, including stock‑specific weakness after removing market moves.
  • Finds similar past days for that same stock (not other stocks) and summarizes outcomes after 1, 3, and 5 years.
  • Produces three headline numbers:
    • Rebound Score (0–100): higher = historically better rebounds after similar past setups.
    • Confidence (0–100): higher = more trustworthy (more examples + more diverse market conditions + stable behavior).
    • Stability (0–100): higher = the score doesn’t swing wildly under reasonable small tweaks.
This is statistics on historical behavior — not a forecast and not advice. Any stock can keep falling.

Evidence (top‑10% washout days vs normal days)

Washout days = the top 10% of Washout Meter days for that same stock (the greenest days in its own history).
Normal days = all historical days for that stock (baseline: “pick a random day”).
Each ticker card shows this evidence side‑by‑side for 1Y / 3Y / 5Y.
